Mars



Mars ,Discovered by NASA in 1964. The fourth planet in the Sun due to the abundance of iron on the planet's surface, which makes its surface appear with a characteristic red color commonly known as the 'Red Planet'

Distance: 1,524AU (227.9 MILLION KM)

Orbit: 687 EARTH DAYS

Diameter: 6787 KM

Atmosphere: -123°C TO 37°C
