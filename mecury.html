Mercury



Mercury was discovered in 1631. Discovered by Thomas Harriott and Galileo Galilei. The planet Mercurius was named by the Romans. In Greek mythology, the name of this god was Hermes (Ερμής).

Distance: 0,39AU(4.878KM)

Orbit: 88 Earth day

Diameter: 4,878 km

Atmosphere: -185C to 425C
